AURA Health AI

AURA Health AI is an AI-powered healthcare prediction system that analyzes patient data to predict chronic diseases such as Diabetes, Kidney Disease, and Heart Disease. The platform integrates Machine Learning, Explainable AI (XAI), and feature optimization techniques to improve prediction accuracy and assist medical decision-making.

Features

  • Prediction of Diabetes, Kidney Disease, and Heart Disease
  • Machine Learning-based disease analysis
  • Explainable AI using SHAP
  • Feature optimization with Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO)
  • Interactive and responsive web interface

Key Skills
